# Domain & Hosting Options

## Domain Registration Sites (to check availability)
- **Namecheap.com** - Usually cheapest, around $8-15/year for .com
- **GoDaddy.com** - Popular but can be pricey
- **Google Domains** (now Squarespace Domains) - Clean interface
- **Porkbun.com** - Very affordable, good reputation

### Domain Ideas to Check:
- bylerandsonsgarage.com
- bylerandsons.com
- bylersgarage.com
- rentabay.com (if available and he likes it)

## The Professional Option (RECOMMENDED)
**Custom Domain + Free Hosting**

### Cost Breakdown:
- Domain: $10-15/year (less than $2/month!)
- Hosting: FREE options available

### Why This Is Better:
- Professional appearance: "bylerandsonsgarage.com"
- Easy to remember and share
- Better for Google search results
- Can put on business cards, flyers, signage
- Looks legitimate and established
- No ugly URLs to confuse customers

### Free Hosting Options (that work with custom domains):
1. **GitHub Pages** (free, reliable, easy to use)
2. **Netlify** (free tier, very easy)
3. **Vercel** (free tier, modern)
4. **Cloudflare Pages** (free, fast)

## The "Free Everything" Option
**Free hosting with free subdomain**

### Free Options:
- GitHub Pages: `bylerandsons.github.io`
- Netlify: `bylerandsons.netlify.app`
- Vercel: `bylerandsons.vercel.app`
- InfinityFree: Various free domains

### The QR Code Solution:
You're right - with a QR code, the URL doesn't matter as much!
- Print QR code on business cards, flyers, at the shop
- Customers scan and go directly to site
- No typing needed
- Works with ANY URL (even the weird free ones)

### However, Consider:
- Word of mouth: "Check out bylerandsonsgarage.com" sounds better than "scan my QR code"
- Phone conversations: Easier to say "dot com" than spell out a long URL
- Professional image: Custom domain shows you're established

## My Recommendation:
**Buy the domain ($10-15/year) + Use free hosting + ALSO use QR codes**

Best of both worlds:
- Professional domain for credibility
- Free hosting to save money
- QR codes for convenience at the shop
- Total cost: ~$1/month for the domain
