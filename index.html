FAQ

Do I need an appointment?

Yes, we are appointment only. Call or email us to reserve your bay before showing up.

What's included with the bay rental?

Every rental includes access to a vehicle lift and air compressor. That's what we've got. Come prepared with everything else you need.

Do I need to bring my own tools?

Yes. We are a bring-your-own-tools (BYOT) shop. Come loaded up and ready to work.

Is there a minimum rental time?

Yes, there is a 1 hour minimum. Plan your project ahead of time so you can make the most of your bay time.

What are your hours?

We're open Saturday and Sunday, 1:00 PM to 4:00 PM. Closed Monday through Friday.

How do I book?

Call or email us to reserve your slot.
